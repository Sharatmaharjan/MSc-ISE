## Possible Future Exam Questions (Based on Syllabus & Trends)

### Unit 1: Foundation of Information System

1. Explain the components of information system. Differentiate between data and information with examples. **(Not asked yet fully)**
2. What is information as a resource? Explain its characteristics and importance in organization.
3. Compare and contrast TPS, MIS, DSS, and ESS with suitable examples. **(Partially asked in 2017 as MIS vs TPS)**
4. Explain the role of MIS in managerial decision making with a real-world case.

### Unit 2: MIS in a Digital Firm

1. What is Business Process Re-engineering (BPR)? Explain its relationship with MIS with example. (Asked as short note in 2015 – possible full question)
2. Explain the development process of MIS using SDLC. Draw diagram.
3. What is strategic design of MIS? Explain with Porter's value chain analysis. **(Not yet asked)**
4. Contemporary approaches to MIS – cloud, mobile, AI – explain any two. (Asked in 2016 as contemporary approaches)

### Unit 3: Information Systems, Organizations, Management & Strategy

1. Explain the changing role of information systems in organizations over time.
2. How do information systems support business strategy? Explain with Porter's competitive forces model.
3. What is the relationship between managers, decision making, and information systems? Explain with Mintzberg's managerial roles.

### Unit 4: Information Technology Infrastructure

1. What is virtualization? Explain its benefits in modern IT infrastructure. **(Not asked yet)**
2. Explain the OSI model and TCP/IP model with layers and functions. (Data communication short note in 2022 – possible full question)
3. What is ETL process? Explain with diagram in context of data warehouse.
4. Compare star schema and snowflake schema in data warehousing. **(Not asked yet)**
5. What is data lake? How is it different from data warehouse?

### Unit 5: Applications of MIS

1. Explain the applications of MIS in manufacturing sector with examples. **(Not yet asked – only service sector in papers)**
2. What is Knowledge Management System (KMS)? Explain its components and benefits. (Short note in 2016 – possible full question)
3. Explain how MIS is used for monitoring organizational performance using Balanced Scorecard.
4. Describe the role of Decision Support System in healthcare or disaster management with examples.

### Unit 6: Ethical and Social Impact of Information Systems

1. Explain Laudon's five moral dimensions of information systems. **(Not yet asked fully)**
2. What is digital divide? Discuss its social and ethical implications.
3. Explain the concept of algorithmic bias with real-world examples. **(Contemporary – likely)**
4. Discuss the ethical issues related to artificial intelligence and big data analytics.

### Security (Cross-cutting – frequently asked)

1. Explain the CIA triad in information security with examples.
2. Differentiate between symmetric and asymmetric encryption with examples. (Encryption asked in 2017)
3. What is Intrusion Detection System (IDS)? Explain its types and how it complements firewall.
4. Explain the concept of Digital Signature its working and applications. (Short note in 2017 – possible full question)

## Exam Preparation Strategy

| Priority | Topics to Master |
|----------|------------------|
| **High** | Physical view of MIS, Simon decision model, Data Warehouse 3-layer architecture, Firewall & encryption, MIS applications (Finance/Marketing/Banking) |
| **Medium** | DSS components & significance, Ethics & social impact, Client-server architecture, E-business applications |
| **Low** | BPR, Electronic payment systems, Digital signature, Knowledge management (short notes) |

### Answer Writing Tips for PU MIS Exam

1. **Draw diagrams** – Physical view of MIS, Simon model, Data Warehouse tiers, Firewall architecture, SDLC, OSI model.
2. **Use examples** – Banking for financial MIS, Amazon for e-business, Walmart for SCM.
3. **Structure 10-mark answers** as: Definition → Explanation → Diagram → Example → Advantages/Disadvantages → Conclusion.
4. **Connect concepts** – e.g., link DSS with analytical modeling, link Data Warehouse with OLAP.
5. **Include current trends** – Cloud, AI, Big Data, IoT (shows contemporary knowledge).
